My hobby is:
Making Blueprints

I like making blueprints because I like to have something to put my ideas in. It also gives me something to think about throughout the day. Blueprints are important. For example, Leonardo da Vinci (1452-1519) made blueprints for a early version of a plane when that was not even thought of at that time! He did the same for a submarine and other inventions even before they were made!
